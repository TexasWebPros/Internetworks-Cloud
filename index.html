Internetworks Redux 2011 by internetworks

Internetworks, my one man boutique web dev studio with collaborators globally, is morphing yet again for the second decade of the 21st century. We're now using Drupal y'all, and this is our repo for our newly reborn web development and hosting oriented Drupal distribution.

SOON COME MON!

Dependencies

Drupal 6.xx minimum LAMP, MAMP or WAMP stack, coffee, strong... lots of it

Install

Go back in your hole and pull it in on top of you, then plug yourself in. Better Lorum Ipsum, no?
